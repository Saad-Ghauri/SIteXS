Construction Progress Management At Work

SiteX™ is the platform that allows your stakeholders—operations, finance, safety, owners, VDC, architects, trade partners, inspectors, and investors—to gain remote access to your construction sites, and see all details of the current and historical progress in 360°.

Capture

Capture your jobsite in the most efficient and effective way possible. HoloBuilder’s JobWalk App guides the process to effortlessly capture 360° and 2D photos, automatically organized by project, floor plan, and date/time.

View

Explore your construction site in stunning 360° from anywhere, at any time. With SiteX, photos become available instantly, providing you with the assurance that your project is fully documented and recorded for photographic proof of progress. Access images on-demand, whenever and wherever you need them.

Control

Take full control over your construction project with SiteX's powerful tools at your fingertips. Easily compare 360° photos side-by-side with your BIM models, historical images, and project schedules to ensure progress aligns with plans. Annotate, mark-up, and tag photos to coordinate tasks seamlessly, all within a single source of truth.
